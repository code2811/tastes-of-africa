Culinary Regions of Africa

West Africa

Known for bold flavors and staple foods like yam, cassava, and plantain. Popular dishes include Jollof Rice and Egusi Soup.

North Africa

Famous for aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and cinnamon. Try Moroccan Tagine and Couscous.

East Africa

Staples include teff and millet. Dishes like Injera and Ugali are common.
