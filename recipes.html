Signature African Recipes

Jollof Rice

A beloved West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, and spices. Tomatoes and scotch bonnet peppers give it a unique flavor.

Cooking Instructions
  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and peppers.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook down.
  3. Stir in rice, broth, and spices. Simmer until done.
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 can tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers
  • Spices: thyme, curry, bay leaf

Moroccan Tagine

A slow-cooked stew with spices, vegetables, and meat, cooked in a traditional clay pot.

Cooking Instructions
  1. Brown meat and onions in oil.
  2. Add spices, vegetables, and broth.
  3. Simmer in tagine pot until tender.
  • 500g lamb or chicken
  • 2 onions
  • Carrots, potatoes
  • Spices: cumin, coriander, cinnamon

Ethiopian Injera

A spongy flatbread made from teff flour, a staple in Ethiopian cuisine.

Cooking Instructions
  1. Mix teff flour and water, ferment overnight.
  2. Pour batter on hot pan, cook until bubbles form.
  • 2 cups teff flour
  • 2 cups water
  • Pinch of salt

Bunny Chow

A South African street food: hollowed bread filled with spicy curry. Curry powder and chutney are key ingredients.

Cooking Instructions
  1. Prepare curry with meat or beans.
  2. Hollow out bread loaf, fill with curry.
  • 1 loaf white bread
  • 500g beef or beans
  • Onion, tomato, curry powder
